Biography

Chaoyan Huang is a PhD candidate of Department of Mathematics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Tieyong Zeng. She received the M.S. from the school of science, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NUPT), in 2022, advised by Associate Professor Tingting Wu. Her research interests include artificial intelligence and its applications to computer vision (e.g., image enhancement, image segmentation) and image processing (e.g., image denoising, image restoration, and medical image reconstruction). She have published 4 papers in top journals and conferences, including IEEE TMM, PR, IEEE SPL, CVPRW. She received several premium awards, including National scholarship, the honorary title of "top 10 academic stars" for postgraduates of NUPT, outstanding graduates of Jiangsu Province, 1st prize of the 2021 academic innovation forum for postgraduates in Jiangsu Province of "problem driven mathematics and application", 2nd Prize of 17th "Huawei Cup" National Graduate Student Mathematical Contest in modeling (as team leader), etc.
